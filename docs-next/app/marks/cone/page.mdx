# Line + Cone

<Lead>A correlation belief in two clicks. The single datum `{ r, spread }` is a relationship: **r** is the most-likely correlation — a line through the plot centre rotating from −45° to +45° as r goes −1 → +1 — and **spread** is the uncertainty, a fan of sample lines around it. Driven by `rotate` (the angular sibling of `resize`: a pointer angle inverted through the channel scale) on the [probe](/editing/probe) driver, so the line follows the mouse and a click sets it.</Lead>

<ApiReference entries={api} />

<Section id="twostep" title="Relationship, then uncertainty">

Stage 0 rotates the mean line; a click commits r and advances. Stage 1 opens the cone; a click commits the spread. No buttons — the probe driver advances on each click.
